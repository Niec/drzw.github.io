Wang Zhaogui

PhD., Associate Professor of Mechanical Engineering

School of Naval Architecture and Ocean Engineering, Dalian Maritime University

Email: zhaogui_wang@dlmu.edu.cn

Educational Background

Dr. Wang Zhaogui obtained his Doctor of Philosophy in Mechanical Engineering from Baylor University in December 2019, following a Master’s degree in the same field from the same institution in August 2016. His foundational education was completed at Dalian University of Technology, where he earned a Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Design, Manufacturing, and Automation in June 2014.

Work Experience

Since July 2022, Dr. Wang has been serving as an Associate Professor at the School of Mechanical Engineering, Dalian Maritime University. Prior to this role, he contributed to the academic development of students as a Lecturer from February 2020 to July 2022, fostering a rigorous learning environment.

Research Areas

  • Additive Manufacturing: Investigating innovative fabrication techniques that enhance production efficiency and material properties.
  • Mechanics of Composite Materials: Analyzing the behavior and performance of advanced composite materials under various conditions.
  • Lightweight Design: Developing methodologies for creating lightweight structures without compromising strength and durability.
  • Green Manufacturing Technologies for Marine Equipment: Promoting sustainable practices in the manufacturing of marine technologies.
  • For further insights, visit the SLIM research group at SLIMarine.github.io.
