Educational Background

From the tranquil surroundings of Baylor University, I embarked on a journey of discovery from August 2016 to December 2019, culminating in a Doctorate in Mechanical Engineering. My academic aspirations were further ignited during my Master's studies from August 2014 to August 2016. Prior to my international pursuits, I honed my foundational skills in Mechanical Design, Manufacturing, and Automation at the esteemed Dalian University of Technology from September 2010 to June 2014.

Work Experience

As an Associate Professor at Dalian Maritime University since July 2022, I continue to delve into the complexities of mechanical engineering, sharing insights and innovations. Prior to this role, I nurtured young minds as a Lecturer from February 2020 to July 2022, where my passion for teaching and research was met with an enthusiastic cohort of students.
